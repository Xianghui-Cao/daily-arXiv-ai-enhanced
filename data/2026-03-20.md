It uses an ensemble with $m_\pi \approx 230$ MeV and finds that the inclusion of at least one tetraquark operator is necessary for reliable spectrum determinations. Method: The researchers use Hermitian correlation matrices with single-meson, meson-meson, and tetraquark interpolating operators on a lattice QCD ensemble with $m_\pi \approx 230$ MeV.
